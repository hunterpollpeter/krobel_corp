Farrowing Inefficiency

Economic Loss in Pork Industry

Piglets lost to unattended birth

Annual loss for sow farmers in U.S. and E.U.

Three step farrowing notification system

Collect

First, we collect. When the sow is moved into the farrowing stall, our device starts taking thermal pictures of the sow from directly above her.

Analyze

When the device takes a picture, it immediately analyzes the picture using our machine learning model. The result is a decision: does this sow have piglets or not?

Notify

If the sow does not have piglets, the device will move on to the next sow. If the sow does have piglets, the device will send a notification.
